Submission Guidelines

  • Condition: Mint / Near Mint cards only.
  • Language: English cards only.
  • Current Prices: Buylist prices and quantities are subject to change.
  • Sort by Category: Keep each buylist category in its own separate pile.
  • Sleeves: EX / V cards may remain sleeved. All other cards must be unsleeved.
  • Do Not Include: Energy cards, code cards, binders, top loaders, or non-buylist items.
  • Quality Control: Mixed categories, excessive sleeved cards, energies, or codes may result in rejection or return.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about quotes, shipping, payment, freight pickup, and selling Pokémon bulk to BulkX.

? How do I get a quote?

Submit our quote request form with your estimated quantities. We'll review your submission and contact you with pricing, shipping instructions, or pallet pickup options if your order qualifies.

🚚 Do you offer pallet pickup?

Yes. Qualifying collections of 250,000+ cards may be eligible for FREE freight pallet pickup. Contact us before shipping so we can determine eligibility and coordinate logistics.

🚛 What qualifies for free pallet pickup?

Collections of 250,000+ cards are generally eligible. We'll review your estimated quantities, card mix, and location, then walk you through the process and schedule pickup if your order qualifies.

📦 Who pays for shipping?

For standard orders, shipping is the seller's responsibility. Qualifying pallet-sized collections may receive FREE freight pickup arranged and paid for by BulkX.

🌎 Do you accept orders from Canada or Mexico?

Yes. We purchase qualifying collections from Canada and Mexico. For freight pickups, a complete and accurate commercial invoice is required for customs clearance.

T Are Trainers accepted with Commons and Uncommons?

Yes. Trainer cards are accepted as part of Common/Uncommon bulk. Keep Trainers to approximately 25% of the total Common/Uncommon count and avoid excessive Trainer-only submissions.

🏪 Do I have to be a game store?

No. We work with game stores, online sellers, distributors, collectors, and anyone with qualifying bulk quantities.
